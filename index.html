Mana Calculator

This app is to assist players of Magic: The Gathering, a trading card game. In this game, you must choose how many "Land" Cards go into your deck. These Land cards are used to "pay" for more useful cards!

This app will tell you how many land cards to put into your deck, based off of the combined "cost" of your non-land cards, as well as the total land cards you wish to have.
